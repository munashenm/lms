# Fees Collection & Management

Managing school finances and fee collection is streamlined in Smart School LMS.

## 💰 Setting Up Fees

Before collecting fees, you must configure the fee structure.

### 1. Fee Type
Define individual fee components (e.g., Admission Fee, Tuition Fee, Exam Fee).
- Path: **Fees Collection > Fee Type**

### 2. Fee Group
Group multiple fee types together (e.g., "Class 10 Fees" might include Tuition + Lab + Library).
- Path: **Fees Collection > Fee Group**

### 3. Fee Master
Assign fee groups to specific classes and sessions with due dates and amounts.
- Path: **Fees Collection > Fee Master**

---

## 📥 Collecting Fees

To collect fees from a student:
1. Navigate to **Fees Collection > Collect Fees**.
2. Search for the student by name or admission number.
3. Click the **Collect Fees** button next to the student.
4. Select the fee items being paid and choose the **Payment Mode** (Cash, Cheque, Online, Bank Transfer).
5. Enter the amount and click **Pay**.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The system automatically generates a **Fee Receipt** which can be printed or emailed to the parent.

---

## 📉 Fee Discounts

You can offer discounts based on various criteria (e.g., Sibling Discount, Scholarship).
- Define discounts in **Fees Collection > Fee Discount**.
- Assign discounts to students before or during fee collection.

---

## 📊 Reports

Smart School provides detailed financial reports:
- **Fees Statement**: Detailed breakdown of fees for a specific student.
- **Fees Balance Report**: List of students with outstanding dues.
- **Collection Report**: Daily or monthly summary of collected fees.
- **Online Fees Receipt**: Track payments made via the payment gateway.
