# Student Management

The Student Management module is the heart of the Smart School LMS. It allows administrators to handle everything related to students, from admission to graduation (alumni).

## 📝 Student Admission

To admit a new student, navigate to **Student Information > Student Admission**.

### Steps:
1. **Fill Admission Form**: Enter personal details, guardian information, and address.
2. **Assign Class & Section**: Select the academic year, class, and section.
3. **Upload Documents**: (Optional) Upload photos, birth certificates, and previous school records.
4. **Save**: Click the "Save" button to generate a unique Admission Number.

> [!TIP]
> You can also enable **Online Admission** to allow parents to fill out forms via the school website.

---

## 🔍 Student Details

You can search and view student profiles via **Student Information > Student Details**.

- **Search Filters**: Search by class, section, or student name/admission number.
- **Student Profile**: View attendance, fee records, examination marks, and timeline events for each student.
- **Edit/Delete**: Administrators with appropriate permissions can update student records.

---

## 🎓 Student House & Category

Organize students into categories (e.g., General, OBC, SC/ST) and houses (e.g., Red, Blue, Green).

- **Categories**: Define student groups for reporting and fee purposes.
- **Houses**: Useful for sports and extracurricular tracking.

---

## 📜 Student Timeline

Track important events in a student's journey, such as awards, behavioral incidents, or special achievements.

- **Add Event**: Click on the "Timeline" tab in the student profile.
- **Visibility**: Decide if the event should be visible to parents/students.

---

## 🚪 Student Exit & Alumni

When a student leaves the school, they can be marked as:
- **Disabled**: For temporary leave or suspension.
- **Alumni**: For students who have graduated.

To view graduated students, go to **Student Information > Alumni**.
